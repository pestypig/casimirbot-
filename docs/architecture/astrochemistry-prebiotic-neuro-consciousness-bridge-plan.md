## Hard Corrections

The following distinctions are required for correctness:

### 1. Dopamine Is Not A PAH

Dopamine is a catecholamine:

- one aromatic benzene ring
- two hydroxyl groups
- an amine side chain

It is not a polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon (PAH), which requires multiple fused aromatic rings and, in the standard astrochemical sense, is a hydrocarbon family.

### 2. The Early Universe Was Not Initially "Suffused By PAHs"

PAHs depend on carbon production by stars.

Correct statement:

- after the first generations of stars produced carbon and enriched circumstellar and interstellar media, aromatic carbon chemistry including PAHs became widespread

### 3. Extraterrestrial Organics Are Plausible; Dopamine Seeding Is Not Established

There is good evidence for:

- aromatic carbon species in circumstellar and interstellar environments
- extraterrestrial organic compounds in meteorites
- plausible delivery of prebiotic organics to early Earth

There is not good evidence for:

- dopamine itself being a significant meteoritic seed molecule for life
- dopamine being directly inherited from interstellar PAH chemistry

### 4. Pleasure Is Not Scientifically Modeled As Molecules "Self-Organizing To Optimize Pleasure"

Current neuroscience supports dopamine as part of:

- reward prediction
- motivation
- reinforcement learning
- action selection

This is not the same as a physics principle of molecules organizing to maximize pleasure.

### 5. Penrose OR / Orch-OR Remains Speculative

Penrose-Hameroff Orch-OR is a speculative model linking quantum state reduction, spacetime geometry, and consciousness.

It is not currently established neuroscience, and there is no established evidence that dopamine quantum state collapse events produce pleasure.
