## Goal

Define the exact repo patch sequence needed to study whether gravity-related collapse models, microtubule observables, and driven-dissipative time-crystal criteria can be compared inside one falsifiable exploratory lane without leaking into the mature microphysics-to-observables framework.

The correct statement is narrow:

- `particle physics -> effective Hamiltonian -> open-system or transport closure -> measured observables`
- `gravity-related collapse models`, `microtubule observables`, and `time-crystal criteria` may be compared inside that chain
- they are not currently established as one validated mechanism

## Non-Goals

Do not let this packet imply any of the following:

- `microtubules_are_established_time_crystals`
- `orch_or_is_empirically_confirmed`
- `stellar_observables_prove_objective_reduction`
- `curvature_proxy_is_a_validated_measurement_theory`
- `special_relativity_alone_determines_quantum_to_classical_collapse`

The repo posture must remain:

- exploratory for Orch-OR, Diosi-Penrose-style collapse, and microtubule consciousness claims
- diagnostic or reduced-order for any comparison metrics
- non-certifying for any bridge from stellar plasma observables to consciousness or collapse
