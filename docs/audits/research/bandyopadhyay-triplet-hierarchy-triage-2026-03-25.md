# Bandyopadhyay Triplet-Hierarchy Triage

Date: 2026-03-25

## Scope

This audit evaluates whether the Bandyopadhyay-style claim family around:

- triplet-of-triplets resonance bands
- polyatomic time crystals
- scale-free resonance across tubulin, microtubules, and neurons
- possible spacetime-geometric interpretation

should enter the repo as executable or mature nodes.

The decision is narrower than a literature summary. The purpose is to decide:

1. whether the claim family has enough primary-source footing to deserve explicit graph nodes
2. what maturity tier those nodes can occupy
3. which falsifiers are required to stop maturity leakage

## What The Sources Support

The primary Bandyopadhyay-group papers are enough to support explicit exploratory nodes for:

- `triplet-of-triplets resonance band` as a reported morphology or spectral pattern
- `polyatomic time crystal` as a named hypothesis family in that literature
- `scale-free cross-scale resonance hierarchy` as a claimed organizational motif

The papers provide direct published wording for:

- triplet-of-triplets spectral organization
- cross-scale comparisons between tubulin, microtubules, and neurons
- multi-clock or polyatomic time-crystal language

That is enough to justify a graph-visible exploratory sub-lane.

## What The Sources Do Not Support

The reviewed sources do not justify promotion to diagnostic or certified tier for any of the following:

- `triplets_of_triplets_intrinsic_to_spacetime_geometry`
- `microtubules_are_established_time_crystals_by_standard_criteria`
- `triplet_hierarchy_proves_orch_or`
- `triplet_hierarchy_bridges_stellar_observables_to_consciousness`

Reasons:

1. the Bandyopadhyay-group sources do not satisfy the stricter time-crystal criteria used in the mainstream DTC and dissipative-time-crystal literature
2. the papers do not provide an accepted geometry observable linking the triplet hierarchy to spacetime structure
3. the papers do not provide independent replication strong enough for maturity promotion
4. the papers remain far from establishing a bridge into stellar observables or consciousness evidence

## Decision

Add the claim family only as an exploratory sub-lane with explicit falsifiers.

Admit as exploratory hypothesis or observable-pattern nodes:

- `physics-quantum-semiclassical-triplet-band-resonance-observable`
- `physics-quantum-semiclassical-scale-free-resonance-hierarchy-hypothesis`
- `physics-quantum-semiclassical-polyatomic-time-crystal-hypothesis`
- `physics-quantum-semiclassical-triplet-of-triplets-hypothesis`
- `physics-quantum-semiclassical-spacetime-triplet-geometry-hypothesis`

Do not admit above exploratory tier:

- `triplets_of_triplets_intrinsic_to_spacetime_geometry`
- `microtubule_triplet_hierarchy_is_a_time_crystal_by_standard_criteria`
- `triplet_resonance_over_12_orders_proves_orch_or`

## Required Falsifiers

If the new sub-lane is added, it must include:

- `triplet_band_pattern_not_time_crystal_without_subharmonic_locking`
- `triplet_band_pattern_not_time_crystal_without_robustness_window`
- `scale_free_resonance_not_spacetime_geometry_without_geometry_observable`
- `polyatomic_time_crystal_claim_requires_independent_replication`
- `triplet_hierarchy_not_consciousness_evidence`
- `no_validated_bridge_from_triplet_hierarchy_to_stellar_observables`

## Recommended Repo Posture

The correct posture is:

- visible enough to ingest and trace
- constrained enough to remain exploratory
- directly paired with mainstream time-crystal criteria and no-go boundaries

This means the repo may compare:

- reported triplet-band observables
- time-crystal signature criteria
- overlap or mismatch between the two

It may not conclude:

- that the triplet hierarchy is intrinsic to spacetime geometry
- that microtubules are established time crystals
- that this hierarchy upgrades Orch-OR into a validated theory

## Result

Decision: `Add as exploratory sub-lane only, with hard falsifiers.`
