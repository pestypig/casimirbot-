## Source Notes

- ESA, *ISO detects benzene in space*
  - https://sci.esa.int/web/iso/-/25880-iso-detects-benzene-in-space
  - Verified that benzene was first detected in interstellar space and that the page ties aromatic compounds to carbon-rich evolved stars and carbon production in stellar cores.

- ESA, *Stellar Radiation & Stellar Types*
  - https://sci.esa.int/web/education/-/35774-stellar-radiation-stellar-types
  - Verified hydrostatic equilibrium on the main sequence and helium-to-carbon evolution in red giants.

- NASA, *Key Building Block for Organic Molecules Discovered in Meteorites*
  - https://www.nasa.gov/solar-system/key-building-block-for-organic-molecules-discovered-in-meteorites/
  - Verified stable prebiotic organics in carbon-rich meteorites and the potential role of meteorites in prebiotic chemistry.

- NCBI Bookshelf, *Dopamine*
  - https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/books/NBK535451/
  - Verified dopamine biosynthesis and central reward-seeking behavior.

- NCBI Bookshelf, *The Biogenic Amines*
  - https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK11035/
  - Verified dopamine as one of the catecholamine neurotransmitters derived from tyrosine.
