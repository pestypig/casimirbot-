# Artifact Template: Institution-First Recognition Framework
Artifact ID: artifact-institution-first-recognition-framework
Ideology source: docs/ethos/ideology.json

Purpose
- Tie external recognition and normalization to auditable process benchmarks instead of faction branding, exile prestige, or battlefield mythology.

Scope
- Apply when outside powers, multilaterals, or guarantor states are deciding whether to recognize a transitional authority, coalition, or electoral process.
- Covers recognition decisions, technical assistance access, staged normalization, and rollback when process benchmarks degrade.

Minimum checks

- phase_decisions_logged

Evidence links
- Public benchmark ladder and recognition scorecards
- Donor and foreign contact disclosures for recognized actors
- Audit reports for electoral bodies, voter rolls, and technical assistance
- Decision log entries for recognition, suspension, or rollback

Review cadence
- Before each recognition phase and monthly while normalization or assistance remains active.

Required sections
- Public benchmark ladder for phased recognition
- Firewalls between institutional support and factional favoritism
- Disclosure rules for donors, foreign contacts, and intermediaries
- Audit, rollback, suspension, and re-entry triggers
- Decision log, review dates, and sunset conditions
