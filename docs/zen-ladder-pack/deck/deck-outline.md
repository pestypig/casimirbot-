# Entrepreneur Ladder + Curiosity Dividend Deck Outline

## 1. Why now
- Implementation cost collapsed
- Trust, verification, and integration are the bottlenecks

## 2. The opportunity gap
- $5k to $100k cliff for new founders
- Opaque rules and insider advantage

## 3. The core move
- Values -> checks -> gates -> receipts
- Every decision is auditable

## 4. Zen handrails as eligibility
- Integrity and safety are a floor
- Pre-rung gate before scoring

## 5. Universal rubric
- Demand, capability, integrity and safety, sustainability
- Same axes across every rung

## 6. Rungs R0 to R6
- What each rung proves
- What support unlocks

## 7. Curiosity Dividend
- Reward verified exploration and public goods
- Additive credits, not rung substitution

## 8. Gates and verification culture
- Two-key approval for high-impact decisions
- Audit sampling and revocation triggers

## 9. AI role
- Lighthouse and clipboard
- Not the judge

## 10. Utility report
- Pass rates, evidence freshness, review cadence
- Outcomes, incidents, corrections

## 11. Anti-bureaucracy design
- Few checks, measurable
- Risk-based audits
- Sunset cycles

## 12. Pilot plan
- Registry + validator
- R0-R2 pilot with receipts
- Expand to R3-R6


> This material reports diagnostic/reduced-order readiness signals and governance guardrails. It does not claim warp propulsion feasibility or near-term deployment.
