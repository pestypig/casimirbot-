# Zen Society Alignment (Draft)

This document captures the policy deliverables and verification hooks for the
six pillars listed in task.md. It is a draft alignment pack; implementation and
operations are tracked elsewhere.

## 1) Protect civilian dignity and prevent predation
Intent: protect civilians, prevent abuse, and provide credible accountability.

Required artifacts:
- Dignity and non-predation charter (public commitment and enforcement scope).
- Reporting channels protocol (multi-channel intake, anonymity option, response
  SLA, and independent follow-up).
- Discipline and repair ladder (investigation stages, sanctions, restitution).

Minimum checks:
- Reports acknowledged within 48 hours with a case ID.
- Independent reviewer publishes monthly stats on reports and outcomes.
- Predation prohibitions are explicit and enforceable.

Ideology anchors: jurisdictional-floor, right-speech-infrastructure,
restorative-harm-repair, capture-resistance, no-bypass-guardrail.

## 2) Stabilize essential services (power, water, fuel, transport, telecom)
Intent: keep lifeline services running before any other promises.

Required artifacts:
- Interbeing dependency map covering power, water, fuel, transport, telecom.
- Essential services priority list with facility contacts and minimum targets.
- Restoration schedule with dates, dependencies, and contingency triggers.

Minimum checks:
- Hospitals, water, and fuel distribution have documented backup plans.
- Restoration schedule is published weekly with a delta log.
- Single-point-of-failure risks are logged with owners.

Ideology anchors: interbeing-systems, stewardship-ledger,
capability-ambition-gradient, right-speech-infrastructure.

## 3) Establish legible, durable authority for contracts
Intent: ensure agreements remain valid across political shifts.

Required artifacts:
- Legal basis and mandate limits memo (public).
- Authorized signatory registry with versioning and contact points.
- Dispute resolution path with timelines and escalation rules.
- Contract provenance record (registry entry or hash).

Minimum checks:
- Each contract references the legal basis and signatory registry version.
- Dispute queue is visible with SLA metrics.
- Amendments follow the published procedure.

Ideology anchors: jurisdictional-floor, two-key-approval, provenance-protocol,
civic-memory-continuity, impermanence-by-design.

## 4) Reopen lawful finance and insurance rails
Intent: restore lawful, monitored flows to reduce coercion and corruption risk.

Required artifacts:
- Sanctions and compliance guidance for payments, insurance, and shipping.
- Monitored payment rail policy (KYC/AML, reporting, audit retention).
- Insurance and shipping risk framework with transparency rules.

Minimum checks:
- Payment rails log transactions with audit retention.
- Suspicious activity reporting path is defined and used.
- Compliance guidance is versioned with change control notes.

Ideology anchors: jurisdictional-floor, external-interface-integrity,
capture-resistance, provenance-protocol.

## 5) Earn local buy-in for a legitimate transition
Intent: convert order into consent through inclusive governance.

Required artifacts:
- Civic governance charter with representation rules.
- Protected civic space policy (assembly, speech, press).
- Transition timeline with milestones and review cycles.
- Grievance and mediation path.

Minimum checks:
- Local councils meet on a published schedule with minutes.
- Civic access and safety requirements are documented.
- Timeline updates include rationale and next review date.

Ideology anchors: sangha-architecture, voice-integrity, values-over-images,
skillful-mediation, impermanence-by-design.

## 6) Keep verification and accountability loops active
Intent: keep integrity loops running with public indicators and corrections.

Required artifacts:
- Public integrity indicators dashboard (metrics and targets).
- Independent monitoring agreements and access rules.
- Correction trigger protocol with escalation and public notice.

Minimum checks:
- Indicators updated at least monthly.
- Investigations launched within defined SLA after triggers.
- Corrections published with causal analysis.

Ideology anchors: verification-checklist, feedback-loop-hygiene,
integrity-protocols, liveness-or-it-didnt-happen, restorative-harm-repair.
