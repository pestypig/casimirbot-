# Artifact Template: External Support Integrity Framework
Artifact ID: artifact-external-support-integrity-framework
Ideology source: docs/ethos/ideology.json

Purpose
- Keep foreign and multilateral support transparent, bounded, and non-capturing while preserving local ownership of the transition process.

Scope
- Apply to technical election assistance, humanitarian support, monitoring funds, communications support, stabilization finance, and institution-building aid from states, multilaterals, foundations, or NGOs.
- Covers donors, intermediaries, recipient institutions, procurement actors, and oversight bodies.

Evidence links
- Donor registry and foreign contact disclosures
- Procurement, subgrant, and pass-through audit trail
- Conflict-of-interest, recusal, and exception logs
- Termination notices, sunset reviews, and corrective actions

Review cadence
- Monthly during active support flows and before any new funding tranche or assistance expansion.

Required sections
- Authorized support types and prohibited influence channels
- Donor, intermediary, and foreign contact disclosure rules
- Procurement, subgrant, and pass-through audit requirements
- Conflict-of-interest, recusal, and termination procedures
- Data handling, reporting cadence, and correction log
