# Artifact Template: Survivable Opposition Framework
Artifact ID: artifact-survivable-opposition-framework
Ideology source: docs/ethos/ideology.json

Purpose
- Make losing politically survivable so elections and negotiated transfers do not default to street blood, prison systems, or militia coercion.

Scope
- Apply in transitional or post-crackdown settings where reprisals, arbitrary detention, minority targeting, or political intimidation make peaceful competition unstable.
- Covers candidates, parties, lower-level functionaries, minorities, dissidents, legal aid actors, and dispute-resolution bodies.

Evidence links

Review cadence
- Weekly during active transition periods and within 24 hours after major retaliation, detention, or intimidation events.

Required sections

- Nonviolent dispute path from complaint to mediation to adjudication
- Retaliation triggers, protection measures, and public reporting cadence
