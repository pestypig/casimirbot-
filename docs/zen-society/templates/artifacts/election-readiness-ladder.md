# Artifact Template: Election Readiness Ladder
Artifact ID: artifact-election-readiness-ladder
Ideology source: docs/ethos/ideology.json

Purpose
- Stage electoral legitimacy from local service delivery and contestable readiness before provincial or national office contests expand.

Scope
- Apply in transitions where opposition leadership is fragmented, administrative capacity is uneven, or the public trust floor is too weak for an immediate national contest.
- Covers electoral management bodies, municipal and provincial authorities, observers, debate operators, civic councils, and technical assistance providers.

Evidence links
- Stage-gate scorecards and advancement receipts
- Service continuity reports for utilities, food, health, and courts
- Voter roll audit notes, observer training logs, and debate access records
- Dispute drill logs, mediation outcomes, and rollback decisions

Review cadence
- Before each rung advancement and every 30 days during active election preparation.

Required sections
- Stage definitions for municipal, provincial, and national phases
- Service delivery baseline and public utility readiness criteria
- Voter roll, observer, debate, and secure communications prerequisites
- Dispute drills, appeals path, and recount or replay triggers
- Advancement receipt, rollback triggers, and public rationale archive
