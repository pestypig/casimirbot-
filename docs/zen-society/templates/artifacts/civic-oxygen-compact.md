# Artifact Template: Civic Oxygen Compact
Artifact ID: artifact-civic-oxygen-compact
Ideology source: docs/ethos/ideology.json

Purpose
- Restore the minimum civic conditions required for credible public truth, protected dissent, and meaningful electoral or transitional participation.

Scope
- Apply when communications blackouts, platform blocking, assembly restrictions, privacy violations, or monitor exclusion make normal campaigning or public oversight non-credible.
- Covers public authorities, telecom operators, humanitarian monitors, human rights observers, election support bodies, and civic intermediaries.

NodeRefs
- jurisdictional-floor

- verification-checklist
- truth-convergence-pathways

Minimum checks
- communications_uptime_published
- shutdown_orders_logged
- assembly_safety_protocol_active
- monitor_access_channels_open
- privacy_preserving_reporting_enabled
- detention_review_sla_defined

Evidence links
- Telecom uptime logs, outage notices, and restoration orders
- Assembly permits, dispersal orders, and incident summaries
- Monitor access approvals, denials, and response times
- Political detention review ledger and release escalation log

Review cadence
- Daily during active restrictions; weekly once baseline access is restored.

Required sections
- Communications restoration floor and outage exception rules
- Protected assembly baseline and dispersal review path
- Humanitarian and human rights monitor access conditions
- Political detention halt, review, and release escalation triggers
- Privacy and online rights safeguards with correction log
