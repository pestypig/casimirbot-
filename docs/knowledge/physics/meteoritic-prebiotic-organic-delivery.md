Definition: Meteoritic prebiotic organic delivery is the transport of stable organic molecules formed in extraterrestrial environments into meteorites and related bodies that can contribute to the prebiotic inventory on a planet.
Maturity: diagnostic.
Key questions: Is the source about extraterrestrial organic molecules in meteorites, not direct life or neurotransmitter seeding?
Notes: This node is limited to delivery and preservation of organics. It does not claim that any specific neurotransmitter was imported intact.
