# Room Temperature Quantum Coherence Benchmark

Status: exploratory
Node id: `physics-quantum-semiclassical-room-temperature-quantum-benchmark-definition`
Lane: quantum-semiclassical

This is a benchmark only; it does not claim microtubules or brains already satisfy the benchmark.

## Exploratory claim
Room-temperature quantum coherence is treated as a benchmark problem: the repo compares candidate microtubule observables against known room-temperature coherence examples before inferring anything stronger.

## Math hook
The benchmark compares coherence times, transport length, and damping against thermal noise floors.

## Falsifiers
- coherence is not reproducible at biological temperatures
- the candidate signal is weaker than thermal or dephasing noise
- the benchmark is used to assert a biological time crystal without sufficient evidence
