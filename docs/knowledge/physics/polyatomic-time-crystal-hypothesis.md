# Polyatomic Time Crystal Hypothesis

Status: exploratory
Node id: `physics-quantum-semiclassical-polyatomic-time-crystal-hypothesis`
Lane: quantum-semiclassical

This is an exploratory hypothesis about coupled resonant subsystems, not a certified biological time crystal.

## Exploratory claim
A polyatomic time crystal is treated as a hypothesis about coupled resonant subsystems, and it is only admissible if a genuine subharmonic response and robustness window are measured.

## Falsifiers
- the system does not show subharmonic locking
- the purported order vanishes under modest perturbations
- the effect cannot be separated from ordinary resonance or harmonics
