# Pulsar Vacuum-Gap Sparking

Vacuum-gap sparking is modeled here as a closure/forcing descriptor and associated limit probe. It is not treated as a universal emission theorem.

## Contract Semantics

- forcing-side references: `gap_electric_field_ref`, `particle_injection_ref`
- closure-side references: `vacuum_gap_model_ref`, `pair_cascade_model_ref`
- limit probes describe threshold status and evidence, then remain claim-tier bounded

## Research Basis

- Surface topography/mountain influence on gap sparking has been proposed as one mechanism for activity below typical death-line expectations: [arXiv:2506.12305](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.12305).
