Definition: The stellar equation of state is the plasma relation that turns density, temperature, and composition into pressure support inside a star.
Key questions: Is the source relying on a plasma EOS, degeneracy support, or an idealized gas law?
Notes: The EOS is one of the inputs that determines which burning regime a star can sustain.
