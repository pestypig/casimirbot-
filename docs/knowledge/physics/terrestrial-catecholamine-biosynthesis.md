Definition: Terrestrial catecholamine biosynthesis is the endogenous synthesis of dopamine and related catecholamines from tyrosine through L-DOPA and downstream enzymatic steps.
Maturity: diagnostic.
Key questions: Is the source about biological synthesis pathways, not extraterrestrial inheritance of dopamine itself?
Notes: Dopamine is a catecholamine neurotransmitter, not a PAH. The biosynthetic path is the relevant bridge, not a direct astrochemical identity claim.
