Definition: Equatorial-bulge torque is the torque exerted by the Moon and Sun on Earth's rotational oblateness, coupling external gravitational forcing to changes in the orientation of Earth's spin axis.
Key questions: Is the source about Earth-orientation torque and axis motion, or about ocean/solid-Earth tidal deformation itself?
Notes: This is the bridge between luni-solar gravitational forcing and axial precession/nutation. It is not the same object as fluid tide height.
