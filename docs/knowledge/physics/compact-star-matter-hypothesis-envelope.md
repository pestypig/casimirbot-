# Compact-Star Matter Hypothesis Envelope

Matter-model explanations are encoded as an envelope of candidate hypotheses with explicit support/contradiction references.

## Contract Semantics

- `matter_model`: normal crust / strangeon / hybrid / white dwarf / unknown
- `status`: candidate / speculative / supported / rejected
- every hypothesis must carry supporting or contradicting observable references
- no automatic promotion to `certified` in phase 1 for speculative matter classes

## Research Basis

- Strangeon matter is framed as a compact-star hypothesis space under unresolved low-energy QCD conditions rather than settled consensus: [arXiv:1904.11153](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1904.11153)
