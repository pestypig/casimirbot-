Definition: Helium burning is the stellar stage in which helium fuses into heavier nuclei, typically after hydrogen fuel in the core is depleted.
Key questions: Is the source discussing the post-main-sequence helium-fusion stage or the earlier hydrogen-burning stage?
Notes: This is the next composition-changing stage after hydrogen burning.
