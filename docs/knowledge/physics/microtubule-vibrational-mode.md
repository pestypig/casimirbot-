## Exploratory claim
A microtubule vibrational mode is only admissible as an exploratory node if it is reproducibly measured with identifiable spectral peaks, linewidths, and quality factors.

## Falsifiers
- spectral peaks are not reproducible
- the observed linewidths are too broad for a stable mode interpretation
- mode assignments change under standard controls
