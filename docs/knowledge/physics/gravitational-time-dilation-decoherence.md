## Exploratory claim
Gravitational time-dilation decoherence is admissible only as a benchmark hypothesis when proper-time differences across branches generate measurable phase dispersion or loss of coherence in a controlled setup.

## Falsifiers
- no measurable proper-time-dependent phase dispersion can be isolated
- experimental bounds contradict the predicted decoherence rate
- the effect is used to infer consciousness or mature collapse without a measurement model
