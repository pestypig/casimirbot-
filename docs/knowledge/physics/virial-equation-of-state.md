Definition: The virial expansion organizes dilute-gas departures from ideality through coefficients B_n(T), with the compressibility factor Z and the Mayer f-function giving the low-density bridge from u(r) to the equation of state.
Key questions: Which density regime is assumed and how is B_2(T) derived from the interaction potential?
Notes: This definition is limited to the controlled low-density route and does not overstate dense-liquid validity.
