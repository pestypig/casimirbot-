Definition: Plasma transport closure is the reduced description that packages collision frequency, conductivity, resistivity, opacity, diffusion, and source or damping terms into a usable continuum or MHD-level model.
Key questions: Which kinetic ingredients were retained, and which transport coefficients or source terms are being closed over?
Notes: This is the plasma-specific closure node that feeds the stellar flare, nanoflare, and sunquake branches.
