Definition: Granulation-driven p-mode pumping is the physical picture in which convection and granular motions inject power into the five-minute acoustic band of the Sun.
Key questions: Is the source discussing p-mode forcing by granulation or convection rather than a collapse clock?
Notes: This is the observational source term for helioseismology. It belongs in the stellar-observables lane and not in the consciousness lane.
