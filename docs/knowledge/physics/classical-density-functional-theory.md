Definition: Classical density functional theory treats nonuniform equilibrium states by minimizing the free-energy or grand-potential functional, yielding relations such as delta F/delta n + U_ext = mu and compatible linear-response limits.
Key questions: Which external potential is being minimized against and what free-energy terms are assumed?
