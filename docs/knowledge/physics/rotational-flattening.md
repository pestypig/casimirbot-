Definition: Rotational flattening is the oblateness that appears when centrifugal support modifies the hydrostatic balance.
Key questions: Is the source discussing rotationally induced flattening or a tidal bulge?
Notes: The flattening feeds moment-of-inertia structure and is separate from the external-tide response.
