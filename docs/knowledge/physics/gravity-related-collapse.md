Status: exploratory

This node keeps gravity-related collapse separate from mature stellar, granular, or consciousness lanes.

## Exploratory claim
Gravity-related collapse models are only admissible as exploratory hypotheses when the collapse scale is tied to the gravitational self-energy of a superposed mass distribution and checked against measured decoherence or collapse bounds.

## Falsifiers
- predicted collapse times do not track measured decoherence or collapse constraints
- no dependence on mass distribution or gravitational self-energy can be recovered
- the claim leaks into mature stellar, granular, or consciousness lanes
