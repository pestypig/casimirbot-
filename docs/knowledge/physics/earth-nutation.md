Definition: Earth nutation is the shorter-timescale modulation of Earth's spin-axis orientation superposed on long-term axial precession, caused by the changing geometry of the Moon's orbit and associated luni-solar torque.
Key questions: Is the source about the shorter-period oscillatory part of Earth-axis motion rather than the long-period precession cone?
Notes: Nutation shares the same torque-on-oblateness mechanism as axial precession, but on shorter time scales.
