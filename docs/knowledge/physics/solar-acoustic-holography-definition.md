# Solar Acoustic Holography Definition

Acoustic holography observables reconstruct source locations/times from wavefield back-projection assumptions.

CasimirBot stores holography-derived sources as `reconstructed_source` records, not as interchangeable replacements for initial Doppler impacts.

This keeps method provenance explicit and prevents conflating direct impact detections with model-based reconstructions.
