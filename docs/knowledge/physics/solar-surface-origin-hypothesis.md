# Solar Surface Origin Hypothesis

Surface-event origin hypotheses link radiative transients to impact and helioseismic observables.

Minimum support rule:

- at least one `radiative` supporting observable
- at least one `impact` or `helioseismic` supporting observable

Timing mismatch rule:

- lag mismatch can downgrade confidence/status
- mismatch does not erase the hypothesis object

This reflects that hydrodynamic trigger mechanisms for sunquake-producing impacts remain contested.

## Source basis

- Early/impulsive timing and mechanism tension: https://arxiv.org/abs/1804.06565
- Spectro-polarimetric constraints and multi-mechanism discussion: https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.07346
