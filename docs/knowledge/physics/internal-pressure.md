Definition: Internal pressure is the continuum support term that balances self-gravity and any imposed external body forces.
Key questions: Is the source using pressure as the support term in hydrostatic balance, rather than as a generic thermodynamic scalar?
Notes: This concept is shared by planets and stars, but the constitutive law differs by regime.
