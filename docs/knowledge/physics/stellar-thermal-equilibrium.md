Definition: Stellar thermal equilibrium is the balance in which energy generation, transport, and loss keep the star in a quasi-steady state over the relevant timescale.
Key questions: Is the source discussing the transport balance or only the hydrostatic force balance?
Notes: Thermal balance is coupled to hydrostatic equilibrium but not identical to it.
