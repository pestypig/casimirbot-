# Microtubule Mesoscopic Observables

Status: exploratory
Node id: `physics-quantum-semiclassical-microtubule-mesoscopic-observables-definition`
Lane: quantum-semiclassical

This node stays observational and does not leak into mature stellar or granular lanes.

## Exploratory claim
Microtubules may be treated as mesoscopic structures with transport, spectral, and perturbation observables, but those observables do not by themselves establish Orch-OR or time-crystal order.

## Falsifiers
- microtubule observables are not reproducible across measurement conditions
- spectral or transport features vanish under controls or noise accounting
- the lane collapses observational transport into a consciousness assertion
