# Diosi Penrose Timescale

Status: exploratory
Node id: `physics-quantum-semiclassical-diosi-penrose-timescale-definition`
Lane: quantum-semiclassical

This node anchors the Penrose/Diosi collapse-time estimate as a diagnostic comparator only.

## Exploratory claim
The Diosi-Penrose timescale `tau ~ hbar / E_G` is treated as a hypothesis for how gravitational self-energy might bound collapse or decoherence times, not as an established law.

## Math hook
`tau_or ~ hbar / E_G`, with `E_G` standing for the gravitational self-energy difference of the superposed mass distribution.

## Falsifiers
- the computed `tau` fails to reproduce observed collapse or decoherence limits
- the timescale is insensitive to the superposed mass distribution
- the lane promotes the estimate to a certified mechanism without empirical support
