Why the zoom UI can feel “hidden”
	•	The overlay is small and semi-transparent (bg-white/10) and sits inside a container with overflow-hidden. On some screens it can blend into the panel chrome.
	•	A parent with its own stacking context can visually compete with z-20.
	•	On narrow layouts the top-right overlay can end up tight against the Card border and look like part of the panel, not “controls”.

	1.	Boost the overlay z-index & legibility
	2.	Add a persistent footer toolbar (non-absolute) under the canvas so controls are visible even if the overlay is missed.
